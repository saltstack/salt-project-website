## We Have a BIG Problem! The Tech Industry Can't Find Enough Talent!

**August 10, 2021**

You’ve seen it happening across all industries–companies are having substantial staffing problems and labor shortages. The tech industry is certainly not immune to this problem. Sadly within our space, it’s even worse.  Currently, there are thousands of high-paying tech jobs that are available and desperately need to be filled!

Why is this problem happening? Is it because of the pandemic or is it because of a larger problem? Tom talks with Chunga about how dire this situation actually is and why he thinks the problem is going to get much worse!  Listen now!
