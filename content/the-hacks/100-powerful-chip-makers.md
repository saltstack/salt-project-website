# The Most Powerful Chip Makers Are??? (Tom Takes a Victory Lap)

Chunga is doing this episode of The Hacks for one reason and one reason only.
It's purely so Tom can take a victory lap!

Recently, Tom sent Chunga a colorful infographic showcasing the world’s largest
chip manufacturers and their market cap.  As Tom predicted on The Hacks a few
years ago, AMD has become a larger and more powerful chip maker than Intel. As a
longtime fan-boy and advocate of AMD, Tom is very excited! In this episode of
The Hacks, Tom doesn't just his victory lap, he and Chunga also talk about all
of the chip makers listed in this infographic like Nvidia, Broadcom, Intel and
others to find out each has become so successful and valuable. Listen now!
