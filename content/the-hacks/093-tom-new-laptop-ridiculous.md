# Tom's New Laptop Is Ridiculous!!

Odds are, if you're working in the tech industry, you're also one of those
people that still gets really nerdy when is comes time to get a new computer.
Tom and Chunga are no different!

Chunga is really jealous today. Tom just purchased a ridiculously cool new
laptop! Plus, it has a new AMD processor that Tom claims is superior to Intel's
i9.

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga are allowing themselves to be gear
heads and talk about stunning new laptop hardware. During his search for this
new laptop, Tom discovered that a lot of claims made by laptop
manufacturers--while not completely false--are indeed very misleading! How
powerful is your laptop? Are you sure?  Listen now to learn how to find out!
