# The Evolution of Video Games

Tom's life has changed significantly over the past few days. Now that he has a
new baby, he's spending more time at home and, in his downtime, he's getting the
chance to play some video games!

For reasons they can't quite figure out, Tom and Chunga haven't talked about
video games for on The Hacks for a very long time, even though it's a massive
segment of the tech industry. One of the games that Tom is most excited about
right now is the new Legend of Zelda game! Experts are saying its going to take
hundreds and hundreds of hours to complete.

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga talk about the evolution of video
games and how incredibly complex their construction and development has become.
They also explore why some video games become legendary, billion dollar,
classics and why others don't stand the test of time. Here's a hint, they both
agree that the reasons have nothing to do with the actual tech.  What are they?
Listen now to find out!
