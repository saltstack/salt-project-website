# What Tom Hatch Would Have Done Different

Tom Hatch has had an incredible life! To be clear, his life has been no bed of
roses, but it has been remarkable.  He's also had an amazing career as an
innovator and thought leader. There are many who would say that Tom Hatch has
changed the world.

Tom loves meeting and talking with people at trade shows, conventions, and while
lecturing around the world. One of the most common questions that people ask
when the meet Tom is "If you could, what would you do differently"?

In this episode of The Hacks, Chunga asks that very question! If he could, what
would Tom change about his life and do differently. You may find the answers
very surprising--shocking even. Listen now to find out what he said!
