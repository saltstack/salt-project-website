# Super Nerd Spotlight: Hedy Lamarr

Every so often, Tom and Chunga like to have an episode that pays tribute to
innovators that have not only changed the tech industry--they've changed the
world. They call these special episodes Super Nerd Spotlights!

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga are showcasing an amazing woman
named Hedy Lamarr! Does that name mean anything to you? Once upon a time she was
a very famous actress, that had become a household name, worldwide.

She was also a brilliant, self-taught innovator! In fact, all of us, nearly
every person on Earth, uses her inventions all day, every day. What was it? What
is this world changing innovation that we should all be thanking her for?
Listen now to find out!
