# Open Source: The Community Is Not The Customer

Tom and Chunga were highly confused by last week's episode of The Mandalorian.
They came away from the story annoyed and wondering what happened!

This confusion reminded each of them about a situation thats currently happening
with Idem Project. In the world of open source software, there are always two
distinct masters that need to be served. There's the open source community, and
then there's the customer. Sometimes their needs align---most of the time they
don't.

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga talk about the respective
challenges of trying to serve the needs of an open source community, while
trying to please the software customers that are required in order for the
project to succeed. Listen NOW!
