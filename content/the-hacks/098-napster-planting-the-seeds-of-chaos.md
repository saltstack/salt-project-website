# Napster, Planting the Seeds of Chaos

Tom and Chunga are feeling nostalgic! Recently, Tom was trying to explain to his
daughters that music used to be fairly difficult to get your hands on. It took
effort and commitment to find certain albums or CD's!

Nowadays, getting music, no matter how new or old is a very easy thing to do
thanks to an open source offering called Napster! Both Tom and Chunga were huge
fans of this decentralized, distributed file sharing platform. Although it was
extremely popular, the very things that made it successful, also brought about
its eventual downfall.  In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga talk about
the seeds of chaos that were planted by the Napster business model and how it
changed virtually everything we do in today's world!  They also talk about if
other decentralized, distributed computing platforms are doomed to suffer the
same fate!
