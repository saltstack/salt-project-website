# Classified Information and 21-Year Old IT Admins

The whole of the United States population has been scratching their collective
heads for about a week now. Everyone wants to know how it's possible for a
21-year old IT guy to have access to the nations most sensitive and secret
information. Some of this information is extremely damaging to the U.S.
Government.

Tom Hatch has a background in the U.S. Intelligence Community and feels a strong
need to apologize on behalf of his former colleagues, who he says are honest,
dedicated and very hard working professionals. He also has an explanation of how
this time of thing happens. Here's a hint, over one million Americans have a top
secret security clearance.

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom answers Chunga's questions about what it takes
to gain and maintain a Government security clearance, and how its even possible
for someone as young as 21 to have access to highly secret, and classified
information. They also discuss the charges and punishment thats associated with
a crime of this magnitude and what--if anything--can be done to prevent this
sort of thing from happening again.
