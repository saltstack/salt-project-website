## VR Headsets, Why Tech Won't Just Let Them Go

Tom is back from phase one of his paternity leave! Chunga couldn't be happier,
to have Tom back. He's also bouncing off the walls because the Vegas Golden
Knights are one win away from the Stanley Cup! Although he hasn't been the
biggest fan of VR headsets, Chunga thinks it'd be cool to watch the game in VR
without actually having to go to the arena.

While Tom was out, Apple unveiled their new "Vision Pro" VR headset that, raised
a lot of eyebrows. In past episodes of The Hacks, both Tom and Chunga have
repeatedly said that VR is in a word--ridiculous. They've long believed its a
passing fad and is doomed to fail, HTC tried to do it, Meta is still trying to
do it, and both companies have lost hundreds of millions of dollars! So why is
Apple attempting VR now, at a time when consumers have shown that they just
don't want it--or do they?

In this episode of The Hacks, Tom and Chunga take a look at Apple's new, $3700
VR headset and tell you why they think it may be a huge game changer that
changes everyone’s perception about VR technology. Listen Now!
