# Open Hour

The Salt Project Community Open Hour is your chance to meet the core Salt
development team and learn about the project. Get the latest information on:

- Salt Project news and events
- Best practices and troubleshooting tips
- Demos of new features or interesting workflows

It's also your opportunity to ask questions and get answers about all things
Salt!
