Salt Project Community Members! 

As we focus on what's most important within the Tanzu platform, we will not be building packages or running tests in our pipelines for Arch Linux, Amazon Linux, openSUSE, and macOS.
This decision helps us concentrate our efforts where they can make the most impact. 

We truly appreciate your understanding and ongoing support!
If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out.

-- Salt Project Team
