Salt Project Community Members!

`pop` and `idem` projects, Python-based implementations of [Plugin Oriented Programming (POP)](https://pop-book.readthedocs.io/en/latest/main/problem.html), have been in maintenance mode since the acquisition of VMware by Broadcom.

**The projects are now considered unmaintained, and are lined up to be archived. All projects will remain public, read-only OSS repositories available under the Apache v2 licensing for anyone who would like to maintain their own forks.**

## GitLab vs. GitHub Project Hosting

All actively maintained repositories for Salt Project only exist on under the [**GitHub** Organization: saltstack](https://github.com/saltstack/).

In the near future, all projects under the following **GitLab groups** will be archived:

- [GitLab group: `vmware/pop`](https://gitlab.com/vmware/pop)
- [GitLab group: `vmware/idem`](https://gitlab.com/vmware/idem)
- [GitLab group: `saltstack`](https://gitlab.com/saltstack) (projects have already been archived)

## Heist and Soluble Archivals

The planned archivals also include `heist` and `soluble`:

- [GitLab: `saltstack/pop/heist`](https://gitlab.com/saltstack/pop/heist)
- [GitLab: `saltstack/pop/heist-salt`](https://gitlab.com/saltstack/pop/heist-salt)
- [GitHub: `saltstack/soluble`](https://github.com/saltstack/soluble)

Support for deploying a `relenv`-based onedir environment of Salt, via `salt-ssh`, [will be available in Salt v3008 LTS](https://github.com/saltstack/salt/pull/66878).

## Discord Channel Archivals

In the [Salt Project Community Discord](https://discord.gg/J7b7EscrAs), the following channels will be archived:

- `#pop-and-idem`
- `#heist`

## Primary Focus

The Salt Project Team is primarily focusing on [`salt`](https://github.com/saltstack/salt) itself. For more details on what we've been up to, and what we are prioritizing, see:
- [Salt Project Blog - Open Hour and Q&A 2025-JAN-16](/blog/2025-01-16-open-hour)
