Salt Project Community Members!

In 2025, the Salt Project website (`saltproject.io`) and documentation subdomain (`docs.saltproject.io`) will be reviewed for potential transformation and content migration.

These changes would be done to consolidate and unify Salt Project with Broadcom policies. More details will be shared over the coming months as more is learned.
