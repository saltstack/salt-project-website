# Salt Project Announcement: Salt 3008.0 LTS is Here!

The Salt Project is thrilled to announce the official general availability (GA) release of Salt 3008.0 LTS.

This milestone marks the culmination of extensive development, testing, and invaluable feedback from our community throughout the Release Candidate cycles. Salt 3008.0 is a Long-Term Support (LTS) release, bringing a hardened foundation, crucial bug fixes, and performance enhancements designed to power your infrastructure with maximum stability.

## What's New in Salt 3008.0?

Whether you are prioritizing long-term enterprise stability or eager to leverage the latest optimizations, this release is ready for production environments.

* **The Tag:** Check out the official ``v3008.0`` tag on [GitHub](https://github.com/saltstack/salt/releases/tag/v3008.0)
* **Release Notes:** View the release notes on [GitHub](https://github.com/saltstack/salt/blob/3008.x/doc/topics/releases/3008.0.md)

## Installation & Upgrade Notes

Users can install Salt 3008.0 via PyPI or through our official package repositories.

* **Package Repo:** Packages are available here:
  * [deb](https://packages.broadcom.com/artifactory/saltproject-deb/)
  * [rpm](https://packages.broadcom.com/artifactory/saltproject-rpm/)
  * [mac](https://packages.broadcom.com/artifactory/saltproject-generic/macos/3008.0/)
  * [win](https://packages.broadcom.com/artifactory/saltproject-generic/windows/3008.0/)
  * [onedir](https://packages.broadcom.com/artifactory/saltproject-generic/onedir/3008.0/)
* **Install Guide:** Refer to our official instructions for [Installing Salt](https://docs.saltproject.io/salt/install-guide/en/latest/index.html).
* **PyPI Source:** Download the package directly via [PyPI](https://pypi.org/project/salt/3008.0/)

| IMPORTANT |
| --------- |
| While Salt supports differing versions between the Master and Minions during transitional periods, it is **highly recommended to upgrade your Salt Master first** to prevent compatibility issues. |
