Salt Project friends, 

Yesterday was my last day working on the Salt Core Team as I am retiring, having reached the age plus a few years.

I just want to thank all of the community for their hard work and contributions to Salt over the 10 plus years I have worked on Salt. I have enjoyed working on Salt with all of the Salt Core team and commercial Salt team members over the years and meeting contributors at Salt Confs and PyCons that I have attended. I wish many years of success to the Salt Team and contributors in the future, especially with the move to using salt-extensions which should help in making Salt
more stable.

Cheers!

-- David Murphy
