This year, a variety of operating systems have reached EOL and newly released. When it comes to Salt support, this means several updates have happened.

Documentation for both LTS and STS:

- [3006.x LTS: Supported Operating Systems](https://docs.saltproject.io/salt/install-guide/en/3006/topics/salt-supported-operating-systems.html)
- [3007.x STS: Supported Operating Systems](https://docs.saltproject.io/salt/install-guide/en/latest/topics/salt-supported-operating-systems.html)

## Deprecated Operating Systems (no longer supported)

- Photon OS 3
- RHEL 7
- CentOS 7
- CentOS Stream 8
- Debian 10
- Fedora 37 and Fedora 38 (only supporting latest Fedora)
- FreeBSD (not officially supported, but is maintained by community members)

## Newly Supported Operating Systems

- Ubuntu 24.04
- Fedora 40
- Rocky Linux 8 and Rocky Linux 9
- OpenSUSE Leap 15.5 (replaces OpenSUSE Leap 15.4)
- SLES 15 SP5 (replaces SLES 15 SP4)

### Supported Operating Systems no longer tested in CI/CD

- AlmaLinux 8 and AlmaLinux 9
- CentOS Stream 9
